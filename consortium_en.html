About Us : Japan Consortium for Adaptation Finance

Purpose and Activities

Organizational Structure

By applying digital technology to predict and analyze adaptation value and providing this information to investors in a quantitative and easy-to-understand format, we will promote the social implementation of adaptation finance, which will drive the expansion of the investment market.

Activities

  • Establishment of financial schemes such as investment and loans, insurance, etc.
  • Examination of adaptation measures that utilize digital technology and formulation of scientific and quantitative evaluation criteria.
  • Acquiring new business opportunities and implementing them in society by measuring the effects and utilizing adaptation measures.
