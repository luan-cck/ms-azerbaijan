Promoting Private Investment in Adaptation Projects: Visualization and Quantification of Adaptation Value through Digital Transformation (DX) and Finance Models

START What is Our Adaptation Finance Consortium?

《Overview of This Exhibit》

In response to the increasing severity of disasters caused by global climate change, we must further promote the implementation of climate change adaptation measures. For private companies to invest more in adaptation measures, it is essential to enable everyone to assess the return on investment in adaptation. Our consortium is working on developing digital transformation (DX) to visualize and quantify adaptation value, which refers to the economic and environmental impact differences before and after implementing adaptation measures.

This exhibit is a simulation that visualizes the impact of disaster mitigation before and after the implementation of adaptation measures using a 3D model. In addition, we introduce examples of adaptation measures that help mitigate disaster damage, as well as the financial schemes necessary for their implementation.
